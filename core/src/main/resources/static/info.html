• Manager:
    • http://localhost:3466/manager/manager (GET & POST)
    • http://localhost:3466/guest (GET)
    • http://localhost:3466/info (GET)
  • Guest:
    • http://localhost:3466/guest (GET)
    • http://localhost:3466/info (GET)
  • Unauthorized:
    • http://localhost:3466/info (GET)
  • GET method:
    • <path> (to get guest page)
    • <path>?all (to get all products from database)
    • <path>?<productName1>&<productName2>&<productName3> (to get products with respective names from database)

    <path> = "/guest" or "/manager/manager"

  • POST method:
    • Must have parameters "name", "organization_name" and "quantity"
